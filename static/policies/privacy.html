Privacy & Data Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services:

Personal Information

  • Name and contact details
  • Account credentials
  • Profile information
  • Travel preferences
  • Payment information

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information
  • IP address and location
  • Browser type and settings
  • Usage data and cookies

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us provide and improve our services:

  • Personalize your experience
  • Process bookings and payments
  • Communicate with you
  • Improve our services
  • Ensure platform security

We Never:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share data with unauthorized parties
  • Use information for unrelated purposes
  • Store payment details on our servers

Data Protection

We implement various security measures to protect your data:

  • SSL encryption for all data transfers
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure data storage
  • Employee confidentiality agreements

Your Rights

You have control over your personal information:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction
  • Delete your account
  • Opt-out of communications
  • Export your data

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site usage
  • Improve user experience
  • Provide personalized content

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.
