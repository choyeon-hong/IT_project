Review Policy

Guidelines for submitting and managing reviews on Wander Critic

Review Guidelines

At Wander Critic, we believe in authentic, helpful reviews that benefit our entire travel community. Our review policy ensures that all content remains reliable and trustworthy.

Key Principles

  • Reviews must be based on first-hand experiences
  • Content should be original and unique
  • Reviews must respect privacy and intellectual property rights
  • No commercial or promotional content is allowed

Review Requirements

Your Review Should:

  • Be detailed and specific about your experience
  • Include both positive aspects and areas for improvement
  • Focus on the location, facilities, and service
  • Be family-friendly and respectful
  • Include relevant photos if possible

We Don't Allow:

  • Offensive language or hate speech
  • Personal attacks or harassment
  • Fake or incentivized reviews
  • Advertising or promotional content
  • Content that violates privacy rights

Review Moderation

All reviews are subject to moderation to ensure they meet our community standards. Our moderation process includes:

  • Verification of user authenticity
  • Content review for policy compliance
  • Spam and automated content detection
  • Investigation of reported reviews

Reviews that don't meet our guidelines may be edited or removed. Users who repeatedly violate our policies may have their accounts suspended.

Reporting Reviews

If you encounter a review that violates our policies, please report it by:

  • Clicking the "Report" button next to the review
  • Selecting the reason for reporting
  • Providing additional details if requested

We take all reports seriously and will investigate them promptly.
