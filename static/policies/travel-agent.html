Travel Agent Policy

Guidelines and requirements for travel agents on Wander Critic

Becoming a Travel Agent

Travel agents on Wander Critic are verified professionals who help our community discover and plan amazing travel experiences.

Requirements

  • Valid travel agency license or certification
  • Minimum 2 years of professional experience
  • Proof of business registration
  • Professional liability insurance
  • Clean background check

Agent Responsibilities

Service Standards

  • Respond to inquiries within 24 hours
  • Provide accurate and up-to-date information
  • Maintain professional communication
  • Keep service listings current
  • Handle client data with confidentiality

Prohibited Practices

  • False advertising or misrepresentation
  • Unauthorized fee collection
  • Sharing client information
  • Discriminatory practices
  • Operating without proper licenses

Listing Guidelines

When creating travel listings, agents must:

  • Provide accurate descriptions and pricing
  • Use high-quality, original images
  • Include all relevant terms and conditions
  • Clearly state cancellation policies
  • Update availability regularly

Commission and Fees

Understanding our commission structure:

  • Standard commission rate of 10% per booking
  • Monthly subscription fee for premium features
  • Payment processing fees apply
  • Commission-free period for new agents (first month)

All fees are automatically calculated and deducted from bookings.

Quality Control

To maintain high service standards, we:

  • Monitor agent ratings and reviews
  • Conduct regular performance assessments
  • Investigate customer complaints
  • Require annual license verification

Agents who consistently fail to meet our standards may have their accounts suspended or terminated.
