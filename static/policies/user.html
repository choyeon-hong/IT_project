User Policy

Terms and conditions for using Wander Critic

Account Creation and Management

Your Wander Critic account is your gateway to our travel community. Here's what you need to know about managing your account:

Account Requirements

  • Users must be at least 18 years old
  • Valid email address required
  • One account per person
  • Strong password required
  • Profile information must be accurate

User Responsibilities

As a member of our community, you are expected to:

  • Provide accurate and truthful information
  • Maintain the security of your account
  • Respect other users' privacy and rights
  • Follow our community guidelines
  • Report suspicious or inappropriate behavior

Prohibited Activities

  • Creating multiple accounts
  • Impersonating others
  • Sharing account credentials
  • Posting inappropriate or harmful content
  • Engaging in harassment or bullying

Content Guidelines

Acceptable Content

  • Travel experiences and reviews
  • Travel tips and recommendations
  • Appropriate travel photos
  • Constructive feedback

Unacceptable Content

  • Spam or promotional material
  • Offensive or inappropriate content
  • Copyright-infringing material
  • False or misleading information

Account Termination

Wander Critic reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts that:

  • Violate our policies
  • Engage in fraudulent activity
  • Create a risk or possible legal exposure
  • Are inactive for an extended period

Users will be notified of any action taken against their account and may appeal the decision through our support channels.
